I wear shoes when I travel.

Enough with all the shit about barefoot wanderlust. A good pair of shoes is something to be grateful for.

About Me

My name is Devin Cooley and I am tired of hearing about your love of dancing barefoot in the moonlight and rain, feeling the sand/grass between your toes. Sure all that spiritual stuff is great, but in my experience,nobody who has to walk barefoot everyday chooses to do so. Shoes are a privelidge and something to be grateful for.

I have been:

  • Columbia
  • Argentina
  • Chile
  • Mexico
  • Costa Rica
  • Fiji
  • Nicaragua
  • Canada
